1. Introduction

Timestamp Saver ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our Timestamp Saver Chrome extension (the "Extension").

By installing and using the Extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

The Extension is available in both free and Pro versions. The Pro version includes additional features that require a subscription, managed through our secure payment system.

2. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our service to you. Here's what we collect and why:

2.1 Information Collected Through Extension Permissions

  • Storage permission: Used to store your saved timestamps, preferences, and license information locally on your device.
  • ActiveTab permission: Used to interact with YouTube video pages when you activate the extension's functionality, allowing us to capture current video information.
  • Scripting permission: Used to inject necessary scripts into YouTube pages to capture timestamps and handle keyboard shortcuts.
  • Identity and Identity.email permissions: Used for Pro license management to bind licenses to Google accounts and enable use across multiple devices.
  • Tabs permission: Used to detect appropriate YouTube tabs and manage the upgrade/checkout process.
  • Notifications permission: Used to provide feedback about license activation, purchases, and important updates.

2.2 YouTube Video Data

When you save a timestamp, we collect:

  • Video title
  • Video URL
  • Channel name and URL
  • Timestamp position (time in seconds)
  • Notes you add to timestamps (Pro version)

This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you use the Pro version with multi-device sync enabled.

2.3 Pro Version User Data

If you upgrade to the Pro version, we collect additional information to manage your subscription:

  • Contact Information: Email address (from your Google account or provided directly).
  • Profile Information: A unique profile ID generated from your Google account or device.
  • Payment Information: We use Stripe as our payment processor. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers, but Stripe may provide us with limited payment information such as the last four digits of your card, expiration date, and country.
  • Subscription Details: License key, subscription status, purchase date, and renewal dates.
  • Device Information: Browser user agent and device identifiers to manage multi-device license usage.

Important: We do not track your general browsing history or collect data about YouTube videos beyond those where you explicitly save timestamps.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Providing Core Functionality

We use the collected information primarily to:

  • Save and display your timestamps for YouTube videos
  • Store your preferences and settings
  • Implement custom keyboard shortcuts
  • Display notes for saved timestamps (Pro version)

3.2 Pro License Management

For Pro users, we use your information to:

  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Generate and verify license keys
  • Enable multi-device usage through Google account binding
  • Send license keys and subscription information via email
  • Provide customer support related to your subscription
  • Prevent unauthorized use and abuse of licenses

3.3 Service Improvement

We may use aggregated, anonymized data to:

  • Improve the performance and reliability of the Extension
  • Fix bugs and address user-reported issues
  • Develop new features based on usage patterns

Important: We never use your saved timestamps, notes, or video viewing habits for advertising purposes or sell this information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

The free version of the Extension stores all timestamp data locally within your browser's local storage. This data is:

  • Only accessible on your device
  • Not transmitted to our servers
  • Cleared if you uninstall the Extension (unless you reinstall and restore from Chrome sync)

4.2 Server Storage (Pro Version)

For Pro users, we store the following data on our secure servers:

  • Your email address
  • License key and subscription status
  • Account identifiers for Google account binding
  • Payment records (via Stripe)

Your timestamp data and notes remain stored locally and are not transmitted to our servers.

4.3 Security Measures

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your information:

  • All server communications use HTTPS encryption
  • License keys are generated using secure cryptographic methods
  • User authentication leverages Google's secure identity services
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure
  • Database access is restricted and protected by secure authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates to maintain protection

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to support our Extension:

5.1 Stripe

We use Stripe for payment processing and subscription management. When you make a purchase:

  • Payment information is collected directly by Stripe and is subject to their Privacy Policy
  • We receive limited information from Stripe to manage your subscription, such as subscription status, last four digits of your card, and expiration date
  • Stripe provides us with the necessary tools to issue refunds and manage billing issues if needed

5.2 MongoDB Atlas

We use MongoDB Atlas as our database service to store Pro user license information. All data is:

  • Stored in secure, encrypted databases
  • Regularly backed up to prevent data loss
  • Protected by strict access controls

MongoDB Atlas operates in compliance with industry security standards, including SOC 1, SOC 2, HIPAA, and GDPR.

5.3 AWS SES (Simple Email Service)

We use Amazon Web Services SES to send license keys and subscription information via email. Email contents are:

  • Transmitted securely to AWS
  • Used solely for transactional purposes related to your subscription
  • Not used for marketing without your explicit consent

5.4 Heroku

We use Heroku to host our backend application that manages license verification and subscription processing. All communications with our Heroku-hosted servers are encrypted using HTTPS.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can access your saved timestamps and notes directly through the Extension interface.
  • Correction: You can edit or delete individual timestamps and notes at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete all saved timestamps using the "Clear All" button in the Extension.
  • Export: Contact us if you wish to export all your saved data in a machine-readable format.

6.2 Pro Subscription Management

If you have a Pro subscription, you can:

  • View and update your subscription details through the customer portal accessible via the "Manage Account" button
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through the customer portal or by contacting us
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting our support email

6.3 Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Free version: All data is stored locally on your device and retained until you delete it or uninstall the Extension.
  • Pro version: We retain your license information and account details for as long as your subscription is active and for up to 12 months after cancellation or expiration to facilitate renewals and provide support.
  • Payment records: We retain payment records as required by financial regulations, typically for 7 years.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13, or the relevant age of digital consent in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will promptly delete this information.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending an in-Extension notification for significant changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Your continued use of the Extension after changes to the Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
